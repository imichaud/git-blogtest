2017 Game Recap


Our innagural game set the tone for everything the DVF game represents. The initial push to plan, organize, and finalize the event was a collective effort amongst a group of dedicated individuals resolute on actualizing the vision for our contribution. We truly couldn't know what to expect in regard to the turnout, but the community rallied and put all of that to ease just by showing up. Players traveled from as far as Austin, Tx to play and put on an awesome contest for the crowd.

The fact that we brought in over two times our goal donation was an astoudning accomplishiment in itself, but we knew the DVF game was here to stay simply by witnessing the fun, excitement, and joy that surrounded the event -- a contribution made by all. Thank you to all who attended and helped make for an awesome evening. Lastly, a special thank you to the players:
