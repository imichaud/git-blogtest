2018 Game Recap


The 2018 game was a strong successor to the prior year, as we exceeded our contribution and attendance. With many players returning from 2017, a heightened level of competition was in the air as Team Gerald Fitzgerald vanquished Team Ramses in a high-flying affair. Thank you to all who attended and helped make for an awesome evening. Lastly, a special thank you to the players:

(Ramses) Dalton Michaud, Trent Tibbits, Shane Morris, Ryan Sager, Jared Martin, Austin Omiller, Sean Shattenkirk, Terron Victoria.

(Gerald Fitzgerald) Sheldon Griffin, Taylor Plimpton, Tyler Foster, Ian Michaud, Ben Gevlin, Joey Werner, Alex Kellard-Ashe, Rob Piano.
