Dana V. Flax Charity Game

The Dana V. Flax Charity Basketball game is a yearly event to raise funds for the Dana Flax Memorial Scholarship -- an award presented to a graduating senior from Ichabod Crane High School. Established in 2017, the game features some of the best basketball talent in the Capital Region. Past participants have included current and former Division-1, 2, and 3 athletes, as well as ex-professional players. As the game grows, so grows the scholarship, effectively amplifying Dana's lasting impact on the community she cherished so dearly.

None of this would be possible without the support of all those involved and the attendees, thank you. We hope you'll join us to celebrate the life and memory of Dana by enjoying a night filled with great basketball, fun, and celebration -- all for a great cause.

Dana V. Flax Memorial Scholarship

Founded in 2008 in Dana's memory, the scholarship is awarded to a graduating senior from Ichabod Crane Central High School who exemplifies the traits and values that Dana embodied as not only a student, but as an individual as well. The recipient approaches life with individuality, an openness to learning, and a sense of humor. Those characteristics, combined with Dana's flair for all things funky and fresh, helped her enrich the lives of all who knew her, and will surely help the award winner in their aspirations.

The award has helped students navigate some of the financial challenges that attending college presents. Since it's institution, eleven recipients have felt the lasting impact that Dana left our community. It's been our pleasure and honor to help the scholarship reach new heights and continue to facilitate its positive impact on those who've been selected and those who have yet to be selected.
